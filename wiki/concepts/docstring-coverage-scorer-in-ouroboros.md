# Docstring Coverage Scorer in Ouroboros

This update to the [[Ouroboros]] project replaces a `real_world` placeholder with a concrete docstring coverage scorer. The new metric quantifies the documentation quality of public callables, providing AI agents with an actionable target for improvement.

## Key Concepts

Docstring Coverage Scorer,[[Ouroboros]] Project,real_world dimension,Code Quality Metrics,AI Agent Performance

## Details

The `real_world` dimension within the [[Ouroboros]] project's scoreboard has been updated to measure the docstring coverage of public callables, including functions, classes, and methods. The score is calculated as the ratio of documented public callables to the total number of public callables, with a current reported score of 0.35.

This change provides AI agents operating within [[Ouroboros]] with a clear, quantifiable, and improvable target dimension related to code quality and maintainability. Previously, this dimension was a placeholder. The corresponding observer dimension references have also been updated to reflect this new metric.
