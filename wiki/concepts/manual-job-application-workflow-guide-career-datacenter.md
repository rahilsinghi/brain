# Manual Job Application Workflow Guide (Career-Datacenter)

This document outlines a comprehensive, day-by-day manual job application workflow designed to generate 10-15 quality applications over two weeks. It details job discovery, research, resume/cover letter tailoring, submission, and tracking using resources from the [[Career-Datacenter]] project. The workflow aims for immediate interview opportunities while concurrently developing an automated agent system.

## Key Concepts

Job Discovery,Company Research,Resume Tailoring,Cover Letter Writing,Application Submission,Networking Strategies,Metrics Tracking,Time Management for Job Search,Application Response Rate

## Details

This guide provides a comprehensive, day-by-day manual job application workflow, leveraging resources and methodologies developed within the [[Career-Datacenter]] project. The goal is to generate 10-15 high-quality applications over a two-week period, aiming for immediate interview opportunities while an automated agent system is being developed in parallel.

### Weekly Schedule (Target: 5-10 applications/week)

*   **Monday:** Job discovery utilizing various platforms:
    *   [[LinkedIn]]
    *   Company career sites
    *   General job boards
    *   NYU Handshake
    *   Wellfound
    *   Built In NYC
    *   YC Jobs
*   **Tuesday:** Research and triage for identified jobs (15-20 minutes/job):
    *   Gathering company intelligence
    *   Fit scoring against personal profile and goals
*   **Wednesday:** Resume tailoring (30-45 minutes/resume):
    *   Keyword extraction from job descriptions
    *   Selection and customization of bullets from a [[Bullet Bank: Reusable Resume Bullets by Engineering Domain|bullet bank]]
*   **Thursday:** Cover letter writing (20-30 minutes/letter):
    *   Personalized hooks and content
*   **Friday:** Application submission and update [[Application Tracking System (ATS) in Career-Datacenter|tracker]]

### Included Components:

*   **Job Discovery Sources:** Specific platforms for finding opportunities (e.g., [[LinkedIn]], Wellfound, Built In NYC, YC Jobs).
*   **Company Research Template:** Structured approach to gathering intelligence (mission, tech stack, unique hooks, existing connections).
*   **Resume Tailoring Process:** Detailed steps for matching skills and experience to job requirements, including the use of a bullet bank.
*   **Cover Letter Structure:** Guidelines for crafting effective cover letters (3-4 paragraphs, under 300 words, specific hooks).
*   **Networking Strategies:** Guidance on warm outreach, informational interviews, and seeking referrals.
*   **Weekly Metrics Tracking:** Methods for monitoring progress and effectiveness.
*   **2-Week Calendar:** Provides time estimates for each activity.
*   **Pro Tips:**
    *   Apply early.
    *   Prioritize direct applications over 'Easy Apply' options.
    *   Emphasize quality over quantity in applications.
*   **Common Mistakes to Avoid:** Insights to prevent common pitfalls in the job application process.

### Expected Outcomes:

*   **Target:** 10-15 quality applications in 2 weeks (~20-25 hours of effort).
*   **Expected Response Rate:** 10-30% response rate, leading to 1-5 recruiter screens.
