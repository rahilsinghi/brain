### `cover_letter_core_paragraphs.md`
This guide provides a modular approach to crafting cover letters, focusing on efficiency and customization:
*   **Reusable elements**: Includes opening hooks, strategies for company research, proof points, and effective closings.
*   **Mix-and-match templates**: Offers flexible templates to create tailored cover letters quickly.
*   **Conciseness**: Emphasizes keeping letters to 3-4 paragraphs and under 300 words for impact.

This document offers practical templates for various networking scenarios:

*   **Coffee chat requests**: Templates for inviting professionals to informal informational interviews.
