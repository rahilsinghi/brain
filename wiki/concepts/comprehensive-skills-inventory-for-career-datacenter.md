# Comprehensive Skills Inventory for Career-Datacenter

This feature introduced a detailed inventory of over 55 technical skills within the [[Career-Datacenter]] project. Each skill is mapped to specific evidence and project IDs, ensuring verifiable proof for resume generation and job applications. This inventory enhances the system's ability to generate accurate and evidence-backed resumes.
