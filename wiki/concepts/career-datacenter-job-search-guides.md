# Career Datacenter: Job Search Guides

This entry documents the addition of comprehensive guides to the `rahilsinghi/Career-Datacenter` repository. It includes detailed resources for crafting effective cover letters, strategic networking, and thorough interview preparation, forming a complete job search toolkit.

## Key Concepts

Cover Letter Writing,Networking Strategies,Interview Preparation,Job Search Toolkit

## Details

This commit (`0f69a32`) to the `rahilsinghi/Career-Datacenter` repository, authored by Rahil Singhi on 2026-02-10, introduced three new documentation files designed to assist with the job search process.

### Cover Letter Core Paragraphs
This guide (`cover_letter_core_paragraphs.md`) provides resources for creating impactful cover letters. It includes:
*   Reusable opening hooks and closings.
*   Guidance on incorporating company research and proof points.
*   Mix-and-match templates to tailor letters effectively.
*   Recommendations to keep letters concise (3-4 paragraphs, under 300 words).

### Networking Templates
The `networking_templates.md` file offers various templates to facilitate professional networking, such as:
*   Templates for cold messages on LinkedIn.
*   Requests for referrals.
*   Follow-up messages after interactions.
*   Scripts for requesting coffee chats.

### Interview Preparation
The `interview_prep.md` guide covers essential aspects of interview readiness:
*   A framework for behavioral questions (STAR method).
*   Strategies for technical deep dives.
*   An approach to system design questions.
*   A list of insightful questions candidates can ask interviewers.
*   A comprehensive checklist for the day before the interview.

These additions collectively form a complete preparation toolkit to support individuals throughout their entire job search journey.
