# Wiki Scanner for Graph Data Extraction

This wiki article describes the implementation of a wiki scanner designed to extract nodes, links, and connection counts from a wiki's content. This scanner is crucial for populating and maintaining a knowledge graph, enabling automated data ingestion and structuring.

## Key Concepts

Wiki Scanner,Knowledge Graph,Node Extraction,Link Extraction,Connection Counts,Automated Data Ingestion

## Details

A new feature (feat(graph)) was introduced to the `[[rahilsinghi/brain]]` repository, implementing a wiki scanner. This scanner is responsible for parsing wiki content to identify and extract key components:

*   **Nodes**: Representing individual articles or concepts within the wiki.
*   **Links**: Representing relationships or connections between these nodes.
*   **Connection Counts**: Quantifying the number of links associated with each node.

This functionality is vital for the automated construction and continuous maintenance of the underlying knowledge graph, enabling systems like the [[Brain-Explorer]] to visualize interconnections.
