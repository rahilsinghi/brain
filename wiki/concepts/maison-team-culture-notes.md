# Maison Team Culture Notes

Maison operates on a first-name basis across all internal communications and technical contexts, emphasizing an informal and small team culture. This includes conventions for branch names, commit authors, and Slack display names, ensuring consistency with the company's ethos.

## Key Concepts

First-name basis,Informal team culture,Naming conventions (branches, commits, Slack),Company ethos

## Details

Maison maintains a strong internal culture of informality, primarily reflected in its **first-name basis** policy. This was explicitly communicated by Fredrik, the CEO, who prefers to be addressed by his first name.

Key implications of this policy include:

*   **Personal Names**: Use "Rahil" instead of "[[Rahil Singhi]]" in all Maison-related contexts.
*   **Branch Naming**: Branches should follow the format `bootstrap/rahil` (e.g., `bootstrap/<first_name>`).
*   **Commit Author**: Commit authors should be listed by their first name (e.g., "Rahil").
*   **Slack Display Name**: Display names on Slack should be simply the first name (e.g., "Rahil").

The team is characterized as small and informal, with a flat hierarchy where even the CEO adheres to the first-name convention.
