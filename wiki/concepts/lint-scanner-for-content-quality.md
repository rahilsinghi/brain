# Lint Scanner for Content Quality

This article details the implementation of a new lint scanner designed to enhance content quality within the `rahilsinghi/brain` repository. The scanner automatically identifies and flags critical issues such as broken links, orphaned content, and various formatting inconsistencies.

## Details

A significant feature enhancement was introduced to the `rahilsinghi/brain` repository, adding a comprehensive lint scanner. This scanner plays a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and readability of the content by automatically detecting several types of issues:

*   **Broken Links:** Identifies hyperlinks that point to non-existent or unreachable resources.
*   **Orphaned Content:** Flags content that is not linked to or referenced from any other part of the system, potentially indicating forgotten or irrelevant information.
*   **Format Issues:** Checks for inconsistencies and errors in content formatting, ensuring a uniform and professional presentation.

This addition aims to improve the overall quality, discoverability, and maintainability of the repository's knowledge base. The changes were part of commit `18f731c`.
