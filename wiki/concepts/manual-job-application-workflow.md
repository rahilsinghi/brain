# Manual Job Application Workflow

This document outlines a comprehensive, day-by-day guide for manually applying to jobs, designed to integrate with the Career Data Center. It details a structured weekly schedule, essential resources, and best practices for discovering, researching, tailoring applications, and networking to achieve 10-15 quality applications in two weeks.

## Key Concepts

Job Discovery,Company Research and Triage,Resume Tailoring,Cover Letter Writing,Application Submission,Networking Strategies,Application Tracking,Time Management for Job Applications,Quality over Quantity in Applications

## Details

This comprehensive guide provides a structured, day-by-day approach for manual job applications, designed to be used in conjunction with the Career Data Center. The goal is to submit 10-15 quality applications over a two-week period, requiring approximately 20-25 hours, with an expected response rate of 10-30% leading to 1-5 recruiter screens.

This workflow is intended to provide an immediate path to interviews while an agent system is being built in parallel.

### Weekly Schedule (Target: 5-10 applications/week)

*   **Monday:** Job Discovery
    *   Utilize platforms like LinkedIn, company career sites, general job boards, and NYU Handshake.
*   **Tuesday:** Research & Triage
    *   Conduct company intelligence gathering.
    *   Score jobs for fit (allocate 15-20 minutes per job).
*   **Wednesday:** Tailor Resumes
    *   Perform keyword extraction.
    *   Select relevant bullet points from a bank (allocate 30-45 minutes per resume).
*   **Thursday:** Write Cover Letters
    *   Craft personalized hooks (allocate 20-30 minutes per letter).
*   **Friday:** Submit Applications & Update Tracker

### Key Inclusions and Strategies

*   **Job Discovery Sources:**
    *   LinkedIn
    *   Wellfound
    *   Built In NYC
    *   YC Jobs (Y Combinator)
*   **Company Research Template:**
    *   Mission statement analysis
    *   Technology stack identification
    *   Identifying personalized 'hooks'
    *   Discovering potential connections
*   **Resume Tailoring Process:**
    *   Precise keyword matching to job descriptions.
    *   Strategic use of a pre-prepared bullet point bank.
*   **Cover Letter Structure:**
    *   Typically 3-4 paragraphs in length.
    *   Under 300 words.
    *   Incorporates specific, personalized hooks.
*   **Networking Strategies:**
    *   Warm outreach to existing contacts.
    *   Conducting informational interviews.
    *   Seeking referrals.
*   **Weekly Metrics Tracking:** Essential for monitoring progress and identifying areas for improvement.
*   **2-Week Calendar with Time Estimates:** Provides a clear breakdown of time allocation.
*   **Pro Tips:**
    *   Apply early in the job posting cycle.
    *   Prioritize direct applications over 'Easy Apply' options.
    *   Focus on application quality over sheer quantity.

*   **Target:** 10-15 quality applications in 2 weeks (~20-25 hours of effort).
