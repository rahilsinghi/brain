# Refactor: Restructure Ideas Section with Power Sequence and Integration Intelligence Agent

This refactor restructured the 'ideas' section within the [[Career-Datacenter]] project, merging existing concepts into a new 'Integration Intelligence Agent' framework. The primary goal was to optimize the conversation sequence for stakeholder engagement, focusing on practical, operational concepts first.

## Key Concepts

[[Integration Intelligence Agent]],Power Sequence (Idea Presentation),Recon (Phase),Schema Normalization (Phase),Drift Monitoring (Phase),AI Discoverability,DataChat/NL Actions,GTM Tracking,Data Schema Normalization

## Details

This commit (`c46b25b`) in the `rahilsinghi/Career-Datacenter` repository significantly refactored the presentation of project ideas. Key changes include:

*   **Integration Intelligence Agent**: Former ideas 1 and 2 were merged into a unified 'Integration Intelligence Agent' concept. This agent is defined by a three-phase lifecycle: Recon, Schema Normalization, and Drift Monitoring.
*   **Idea Reordering**: The sequence of presenting ideas was optimized for an 'optimal conversation sequence'. The 'Integration Intelligence Agent' was prioritized as the initial, grounded, and operational concept, followed by 'AI Discoverability', and then 'DataChat/NL Actions'. 'Ops agents' were positioned as trailing discussion threads.
*   **Reframing**: The 'Integration Intelligence Agent' was reframed to focus on data schema normalization, explicitly moving away from 'GTM tracking' (Go-to-Market tracking), which was identified as being outside the target stakeholder's (Rogers) domain, and more aligned with [[Conor Winders]]'s expertise. The focus was shifted to data infrastructure at scale (1,000+ marketplace adapter level).
*   **Communication Strategy**: The advisor's exact opening line for introducing the lead idea was incorporated. An explicit warning note was added to avoid using GTM framing when discussing with Rogers.
*   **Documentation Updates**: The conversation flow (Section 6) and the Quick Reference Card were updated to reflect the new idea sequence and include per-idea caveats.

This refactoring was co-authored with [[Claude Sonnet 4.6]].
