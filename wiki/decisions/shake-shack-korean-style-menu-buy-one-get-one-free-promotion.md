# Shake Shack Korean Style Menu Buy One Get One Free Promotion

Shake Shack offers a promotion where customers can buy one Korean Style Menu item and get a second one free by using the code SPICYSWEET. The offer is valid from April 9th to April 22nd, 2026, for orders placed through specific channels.

## Key Concepts

- Korean Style Menu
- Buy One Get One Free (BOGO)
- Promotional Code (SPICYSWEET)
- Limited-Time Offer
- Qualifying Menu Items

## Details

Shake Shack is running a limited-time promotion where customers can enjoy a **Buy One, Get One Free** deal on select Korean Style Menu items. To take advantage of the offer, customers must add two qualifying items to their cart and enter the code **SPICYSWEET** at checkout. The discount is applied to the item of lesser value, with a maximum savings of $17.79 per order. The promotion is valid from **April 9th to April 22nd, 2026**, and applies to orders placed via the Shake Shack Mobile App, shakeshack.com, or in-store kiosks. The following items are eligible:

- K-Shack BBQ Burger
- K-Shack Fried Chicken Sandwich
- K-Shack Fried Chicken Bites
- K-Shack Spicy BBQ Fries
- K-Shack Spicy BBQ Cheese Fries
- K-Shack Spicy Caramel Shake

**Terms and Conditions:**
- Excludes drive-thru and third-party delivery app orders.
- Limit one free item per order.
- Taxes, delivery fees, and service charges are not included in the discount.
- Cannot be combined with other promotions.
- Code is case-sensitive and must be entered in all caps.
- Offer valid at participating U.S. locations only (excluding airports, stadiums, arenas, travel plazas, and museums).
- While supplies last.
