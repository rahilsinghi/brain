# Phishing Attempt: Password and Timing Disclosure Scam

An email purporting to reveal a user's password and access timing, sent by 'Urban Monkey®', appears to be a phishing attempt. The email contains suspicious links and visual elements designed to mimic legitimate communications.

## Key Concepts

## Details

This email, sent from 'Urban Monkey®' with the subject 'Your Password and timing revealed!', is a classic example of a phishing attempt. The email claims to disclose the recipient's password and access timing, leveraging fear and urgency to manipulate the victim into engaging with malicious links. The message includes suspicious hyperlinks, an unverifiable sender address, and visual elements that may mimic legitimate communications to increase credibility. The email also attempts to collect personal information by encouraging the recipient to follow social media links and update their contact information. No legitimate organization would request sensitive information in this manner, and users are advised to delete such emails immediately and report them to their email provider.
