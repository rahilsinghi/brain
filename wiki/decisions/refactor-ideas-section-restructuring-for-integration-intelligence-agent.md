## Details

This commit (`c46b25b`) in the [[Career-Datacenter]] repository, authored by Rahil Singhi on 2026-03-03, involved significant restructuring of an 'ideas section'. The changes included:

*   **Integration Intelligence Agent**: Former ideas 1 and 2 were merged into a unified 'Integration Intelligence Agent' concept, defining its 3-phase lifecycle: Recon, Schema Normalization, and Drift Monitoring.
