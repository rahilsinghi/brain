## Details

### Key Developments in Hospitality Industry

#### Royal Orchid Hotels Limited (ROHL) and Hamptons by Hilton Partnership
Arjun Baljee, president of ROHL, emphasized that the partnership with Hamptons by Hilton is a strategic move to fill a critical mid-market gap. This collaboration is expected to accelerate ROHL's development pipeline, leveraging Hilton's brand strength and ROHL's local expertise. The mid-market segment is a key focus area for ROHL, aligning with the growing demand for affordable yet quality accommodations.

#### Civil Aviation Ministry Policy Change
The Civil Aviation Ministry has taken steps to reduce landing and parking charges at non-major airports. This move is aimed at curbing the rising costs of airfares, which have been a concern for travelers. The policy change is part of a broader initiative to make air travel more accessible and affordable, particularly for domestic passengers. The Airports Authority of India (AAI) will implement these changes, which are expected to have a positive impact on the aviation sector.

#### New Hotel Openings
- **Wyndham Hotels & Resorts**: Wyndham is expanding its presence in India, targeting emerging markets with strong domestic travel demand. This strategic move aligns with the company's long-term growth objectives and the increasing trend of domestic tourism.
- **Sayaji Hotels**: Launched The Forest Chapter brand, which offers immersive experiences in natural settings. The brand has opened three resorts in Sasan Gir, Jawai, and Madhai, each tailored to the unique terrain of its location.
- **TreeHouse Hotels**: Announced the signing of a brownfield hotel in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh. The hotel will be a contemporary, full-service facility catering to the evolving needs of modern travelers.
- **Royal Orchid Hotels Ltd.**: Expanded its religious tourism footprint with the signing of Regenta Z - Vrindavan, Mathura. The hotel is strategically located near major pilgrimage sites, making it an ideal destination for religious tourists.

#### Restaurant Expansions
- **Millo**: Expanded to Nagpur, enhancing the city's F&B culture. The new location is designed as a social space that reflects modern connectivity and urban lifestyle.
- **Wow! Momo**: Reached a significant milestone with 850 stores across India. The expansion is driven by sustained consumer demand, fueled by a growing urban population and rising disposable incomes.

#### Industry Appointments
- **Accor**: Appointed Amit Malik as vice president - people & culture for South Asia. Malik will lead talent and culture initiatives across India and the broader South Asian region.
- **Hyatt Regency Ahmedabad**: Elevated Aashish Sharma to director of sales. Sharma will oversee sales strategy and drive revenue growth through innovative approaches.
