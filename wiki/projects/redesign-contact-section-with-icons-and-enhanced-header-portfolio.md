# Redesign Contact Section with Icons and Enhanced Header (Portfolio)

This update significantly reworks the contact section of the portfolio, replacing traditional text links with modern inline SVG icons for email, LinkedIn, GitHub, and resume download. It also introduces a more engaging 'SAY HI' header with a blinking cursor effect and a comment-style subtitle, alongside a fix for oversized link hitboxes.

## Details

The contact section of the [[rahilsinghi/portfolio]] repository underwent a significant redesign. The primary change involved replacing verbose text links with compact and visually appealing inline SVG icons. These icons represent direct links to email, [[LinkedIn]] profile, [[GitHub]] profile, and a resume download. 

Alongside this, a usability issue with oversized link hitboxes, which could lead to accidental clicks, was resolved. 

Further enhancing the user experience, a new 'SAY HI' header was implemented. This header is designed to be welcoming, featuring a blinking cursor effect to simulate typing and a comment-style subtitle, adding a touch of interactivity and personality to the section.
