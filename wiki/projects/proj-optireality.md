# OptiReality: The Optimal AI Solution for Virtual Reality

**research** · Fall 2025 - Present

## Problem

Generative AI models for AR/VR are computationally intensive, causing high latency on resource-constrained edge devices, which degrades user experience in real-time immersive environments.

## Approach

Conducted literature review of state-of-the-art gaze VLM papers and explored novel approaches for efficient generative AI deployment on AR/VR devices. Currently researching text-based diffusion models for real-time edge inference with focus on model compression and optimization techniques.

## Results

Work in progress - conducting comprehensive survey of latest generative AI research for AR/VR applications; transitioning to text-based diffusion model development.

## Key Points

- Conducting research on efficient generative AI for AR/VR under Prof. Sai Qian Zhang's Vertically Integrated Project (OptiReality), focusing on reducing latency for edge-deployed models
- Surveyed state-of-the-art gaze VLM papers and explored novel approaches for real-time situational awareness in virtual reality environments
- Currently researching text-based diffusion models with focus on model compression (quantization, pruning) and real-time inference optimization on resource-constrained AR/VR devices
- Utilizing NYU HPC resources and PyTorch for experimentation with generative AI algorithms including diffusion models and transformers

## Tech Stack

Python, PyTorch, Generative AI (Diffusion Models, Transformers), Vision-Language Models (VLMs), Gaze-based Models, Text-based Diffusion Models, NYU HPC, Edge Computing, Model Compression, Real-time Inference Optimization
