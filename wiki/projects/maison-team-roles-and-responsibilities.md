# Maison Team: Roles and Responsibilities

This article outlines the roles and responsibilities of the Maison team as of April 2026, detailing the structure across leadership, engineering (including AI, backend, and QA), and commercial/sales functions. It identifies key personnel, their primary areas of ownership, and relevant technical expertise or market focus. The content is based on Slack introductions from April 14-15, 2026.

## Details

The [[Maison]] team is structured into Leadership, Engineering, and Commercial/Sales departments, with specific individuals assigned to key responsibilities.

### Leadership
*   **[[Fredrik Sjoberg]]** (Co-founder & CEO, Product Manager): Based in NYC, he owns the product roadmap and prioritization. (Referenced from [[Call Preparation for Maison and Fredrik Sjoberg]])
*   **[[Kim Granlund]]** (Head of Product Design & Strategy): Focuses on the next-gen UI foundation (A2UI) and is technically proficient in frontend areas like SSR, view transitions, and virtualization.

### Engineering Team
*   **[[Sandeep Baynes]]** (Lead Engineer / DevOps): Built the monorepo and is responsible for all infrastructure, including [[AWS CDK]], deployment pipelines, and test/staging/production environments. Manages [[Docker]], [[ECS]], [[DynamoDB]], and other cloud infrastructure.
*   **[[Noel Panicker]]** (AI Engineer): Works on the AI backend, encompassing the LLM service, AI Visibility feature, chat agent, scraper, and pipeline tasks. He rearchitected AI Visibility from Playwright automation to [[OpenAI Agents SDK]].
*   **[[Durgansh Dixit]]** (AI Backend Engineer): Based in London, his work includes QA pair generation, deduplication, [[Pinecone]] vector search, and the LLM service.
*   **[[Javed MD]]** (Backend Engineer): Specializes in Property Management System (PMS) integrations with systems such as [[Mews]], [[Cloudbeds]], [[StayNTouch]], and [[Opera Cloud]]. He handles ARI (availability, rates, inventory) connectivity.
*   **[[Julia Burke]]** (QA Engineer): Manages test automation, the Quality Dashboard, and regression testing. She is also learning to build applications using [[Claude Code Vault Integration Documentation]].
*   **[[Rahil Singhi]]** (AI Engineer - NEW, onboarding April 15, 2026): An NYU graduate based in NYC, with experience in the travel and hotel sectors.

### Commercial / Sales Team
*   **[[David McGloin]]** (Sales): Based in London, he handles hotel prospecting, partner relationships, and AI Visibility demonstrations.
*   **[[Jory]]** (Sales): Manages hotel onboarding, go-lives, client support, and demos. He also works with booking engines and PMS integrations from a commercial perspective.
