# Karen Project: Personality System V2 Ladder Update (Channel Rules & Level Contexts)

This update to the Karen project's personality system focuses on refining channel rules and updating level contexts to align with the V2 escalation ladder. It involved adding email, voice call, and Slack as communication channels while removing LinkedIn and Twitter.
