# Optimum Mobile Customer Feedback Survey

Optimum Mobile sends a survey to customers to gather feedback on their service experience, aiming to improve customer satisfaction. The survey asks customers to rate their likelihood to recommend Optimum Mobile on a scale from 0 to 10.

## Key Concepts

- Customer feedback collection
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Service quality improvement
- Survey distribution via email

## Details

Optimum Mobile, a telecommunications service provider, sent an email to its customers inviting them to participate in an online survey. The email emphasizes the company's commitment to meeting customer expectations and improving service quality. The survey is designed to take approximately 3 minutes to complete and includes a rating scale from 0 to 10 to measure how likely customers are to recommend Optimum Mobile to others. This feedback is used to evaluate customer satisfaction and drive improvements in service delivery. The email includes direct links for customers to select their rating or begin the survey. Optimum also provides an opt-out link for customers who do not wish to receive future survey invitations.
