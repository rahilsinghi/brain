# Amazon Nova AI Hackathon Winners Announcement

The Amazon Nova AI Hackathon winners have been announced, with BackstageCommericals taking first prize and Oraus AI securing second place. Category excellence awards were also given to standout projects in Agentic Systems, Multimodal Understanding, UI Automation, and Voice AI.

## Details

The Amazon Nova AI Hackathon, hosted by Devpost, has officially announced its winners. The event saw hundreds of developers submit projects, with the top two prizes awarded to **BackstageCommericals** and **Oracus AI**. In addition to the overall winners, several projects were recognized for excellence in specific categories:

- **Best of Agentic System**: Sai – Your Computer Can Finally See
- **Best of Multimodal Understanding**: Project Memoria
- **Best of UI Automation**: Title AI
- **Best of Voice AI**: Flare

The **Best Student App** award went to **SpatialMath AI**. Amazon Nova expressed gratitude to all participants for their creativity and effort, encouraging everyone to continue building and exploring the potential of AI.
