# Maison Linear Projects Overview

This article provides an overview of active and completed projects for Maison, categorized by their lead teams and current status. It details key initiatives in product development and design, focusing on areas like AI visibility, sales automation, PMS integrations, and client customization. The document highlights project leads, target dates, priorities, and technologies used for each initiative.

## Key Concepts

Maison Project Management,Product Development,Creative Design,AI Agent Visibility,Sales Automation,Property Management System (PMS) Integration,Knowledge Base (KB) Quality,ChatGPT Apps,Client Custom Themes,LLM (Large Language Model) Applications

## Details

Maison organizes its development efforts under two primary teams:
*   **Product Development** (key: MA) — The main engineering team.
*   **Creative Design** — The dedicated design team.

### Active Projects (not completed/backlog)

#### Maison Sales Agent 1.0 — Discovery
*   **Lead:** Noel Panicker
*   **Target:** April 30, 2026
*   **Priority:** High
*   **Description:** Focuses on enabling proactive sales behavior from chat interactions, including lead capture, experience requests, promo code distribution, and upsell opportunities. The goal is to transform webchat into an active revenue channel. Version 1.0 will not feature live pricing or inventory, focusing solely on lead generation. This is the first project planned and curated by Claude Cowork.

#### AI Visibility 1.0 — Delivery
*   **Lead:** Sandeep Baynes
*   **Priority:** High
*   **Description:** Aims to improve the visibility of client knowledge base content for AI agents like [[ChatGPT]], [[Gemini]], and Perplexity. It leverages the [[OpenAI Agents SDK]] with [[gpt-4.1]] and a WebSearchTool. The project generates reports comparing Maison KB answers against information provided by ChatGPT about specific hotels. Pilot customers include Ameswell, Skylark, and White Sands. A key competitor in this space is Lighthouse (Connect AI).

#### KB Quality Workflow — Delivery
*   **Lead:** Durgansh Dixit
*   **Priority:** High
*   **Description:** Implements test suites, specifically Golden FAQ (GFAQ) testing, to identify and flag gaps in the knowledge base content for operators to address.

#### PMS Integration 2.0 (Reservations) — Discovery
*   **Lead:** Javed MD
*   **Priority:** Medium
*   **Description:** Expands upon existing integrations to allow for searching (GET), managing (PUT), and creating (POST) reservations directly through the system.

#### Maison Travel ChatGPT App PoC — Discovery
*   **Description:** A proof-of-concept project in partnership with Layo to build GPT Apps. This includes:
    1.  **Maison Travel:** A content platform designed for all clients.
    2.  **Park Lane GPT App:** Enables direct booking capabilities within the GPT environment.

#### Client Custom Themes — Discovery
*   **Lead:** Kim Granlund
*   **Description:** Allows clients to customize various aesthetic elements of their interfaces, including colors, fonts, shapes, container types, and overall brand elements.

### Completed Projects
*   **PMS Integration 1.0 (ARI only):** Integrated with [[Cloudbeds]], Mews certified, and has StayNTouch and Opera Cloud integrations in progress.
*   **Website Content Sync (auto-scrape):** Successfully implemented automatic content scraping for website synchronization.
*   **Source Ingestion (docs & beyond):** Completed the ingestion of various data sources, including documentation.
*   **Select Registry Pilot & Full Launch:** Completed both the pilot phase and full launch for Select Registry.
*   **Scraper v1.2:** Version 1.2 of the data scraper has been completed.
*   **Scheduled Analytics Email:** Implemented a system for sending scheduled analytics reports via email.
