# LinkedIn Posts Section Redesign in Portfolio

This update introduces a redesigned LinkedIn posts section within the [[rahilsinghi/portfolio]] repository, featuring new `PostCard` components. It implements image cards with tag badges, line-clamped excerpts, and a responsive 4-column grid layout. The section also includes a dynamic header displaying the post count and a 'View All' call to action.

## Key Concepts

PostCard Component,Responsive Grid Layout,Image Cards,Tag Badges,Line-clamped excerpts,Call to Action (CTA)

## Details

The `rahilsinghi/portfolio` repository received a significant front-end update with the redesign of its LinkedIn posts section. This involved the creation of a new `PostCard` component to elegantly display individual LinkedIn posts. Each `PostCard` now supports: 

*   **Image Integration**: Allowing for visually rich post previews.
*   **Tag Badges**: Overlaid directly on images for quick categorization or highlighting.
*   **Line-Clamped Excerpts**: Ensuring a clean and consistent display of post summaries, regardless of length.
*   **Responsive 4-Column Grid**: The layout adapts to different screen sizes, providing an optimal viewing experience. 

Additionally, a new section header was added, which dynamically displays the total count of posts and includes a 'View All' call to action, encouraging users to explore more content.
