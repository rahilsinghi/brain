# Operational Playbooks for Career-Datacenter

This document outlines the creation of operational playbooks for the Career-Datacenter project, ensuring comprehensive guides for data ingestion and the job application workflow. These playbooks standardize processes for extracting data, analyzing job descriptions, tailoring applications, and maintaining data quality.

## Key Concepts

[[Operational Playbooks]],[[Data Ingestion Workflows]],[[Job Application Workflow]],[[Resume Tailoring]],[[Data Integrity Checklist]]

## Details

This commit introduces two essential operational playbooks for the [[Career-Datacenter]] project, providing structured guidance for system usage and maintenance:

### `ingest_notes.md`
This playbook focuses on the structured ingestion of data into the Career-Datacenter system. It covers:
*   **Data Extraction**: Detailed instructions on how to extract information from various sources, including resumes, Cursor chats, existing repositories, and job descriptions (JDs).
*   **Structured Workflows**: Defines specific, repeatable workflows for each source type to ensure consistency and efficiency.
*   **Quality Checklist**: A comprehensive checklist to ensure data integrity and accuracy during the ingestion process.

### `tailoring_playbook.md`
This playbook provides a step-by-step guide for the job application process, from initial analysis to final submission and tracking. Key components include:
*   **Job Application Workflow**: A complete, step-by-step process for generating and submitting job applications.
*   **JD Analysis**: Guidance on how to effectively analyze job descriptions, including techniques for keyword extraction to optimize resume matching.
*   **Company Research Guidelines**: Instructions for conducting thorough research on target companies to better tailor applications.
*   **Bullet Selection Strategy**: Methodologies for selecting the most relevant resume bullets from the [[Bullet Bank: Reusable Resume Bullets by Engineering Domain]] based on the job description.
*   **Resume/Cover Letter Generation**: The process for generating customized resumes and cover letters using the system's capabilities.
*   **Application Tracker Updates**: Procedures for updating the [[Application Tracking System (ATS) in Career-Datacenter]] to maintain accurate records.
*   **Quality Checklist and Timeline**: A final review checklist and recommended timeline to ensure high-quality applications and timely submissions.

These playbooks serve as complete operational guides, enhancing the maintainability and usability of the [[Career-Datacenter]] system.
