# Pre-seeding Judge Profiles with Aesthetic Embeddings (Flock Project)

This feature pre-seeds six distinct judge profiles within the [[Flock]] project, each designed with specific travel preferences and aesthetic embeddings. The goal is to generate interesting mediator conflicts, leveraging 3072-dimensional embeddings from Gemini-embedding-001.

## Key Concepts

Judge profiles,Aesthetic embeddings,Gemini-embedding-001,Mediator conflicts,Travel preferences

## Details

As part of the [[Flock]] project, six initial judge profiles have been pre-seeded to facilitate specific scenarios. Each profile is approximately 55% complete and includes tailored travel preferences designed to generate 'mediator conflicts' during system operations. The profiles include:

*   **Aayush:** Focused on food exploration.
*   **Thor:** Interested in adventure and design.
*   **Akriti:** Prefers boutique coastal experiences.
*   **Liam:** Geared towards social nightlife.
*   **Gabor:** Specializes in architecture and wine.
*   **JP:** Represents a modern minimalist aesthetic.

[[Aesthetic embeddings]] for these profiles were generated using the `gemini-embedding-001` model, resulting in 3072-dimensional vectors. This implementation was co-authored with Claude Opus 4.6.
