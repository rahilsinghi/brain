# Career-Datacenter: Operational Playbooks

This document introduces two key operational playbooks for the Career-Datacenter project: `ingest_notes.md` detailing data ingestion workflows and quality control, and `tailoring_playbook.md` outlining the step-by-step job application process. Together, these guides ensure efficient system usage and data integrity.

## Key Concepts

Data Ingestion Workflows,Job Application Workflow,Resume Tailoring,Quality Checklists,Company Research,Keyword Extraction

## Details

This commit introduces essential documentation in the form of operational playbooks for the [[Career-Datacenter]] project. These guides are designed to standardize procedures for both data ingestion and the job application process, ensuring consistency and quality.

### `ingest_notes.md`
This playbook focuses on the ingestion of raw data into the system, covering:
*   Methods for extracting data from various sources, including resumes, Cursor chats, existing repositories, and job descriptions (JDs).
*   Structured workflows tailored for each source type to streamline the ingestion process.
*   A comprehensive quality checklist to maintain data integrity and accuracy.

### `tailoring_playbook.md`
This playbook provides a detailed, step-by-step guide for the job application workflow:
*   **JD Analysis and Keyword Extraction:** Techniques for dissecting job descriptions to identify key requirements and keywords.
*   **Company Research Guidelines:** Best practices for researching target companies to tailor applications effectively.
*   **Bullet Selection Strategy:** A methodology for choosing and customizing resume bullets from a [[Bullet Bank: Reusable Resume Bullets by Engineering Domain]] to match specific job requirements.
*   **Resume/Cover Letter Generation Process:** Guidelines for generating tailored resumes and cover letters.
*   **Application Tracker Updates:** Instructions for updating the [[Application Tracking System (Career-Datacenter)]] to maintain an organized record of applications.
*   **Quality Checklist and Timeline:** A final review process and timeline recommendations to ensure high-quality, timely submissions.

These playbooks serve as complete operational guides for both maintaining and effectively utilizing the [[Career-Datacenter]] system.
