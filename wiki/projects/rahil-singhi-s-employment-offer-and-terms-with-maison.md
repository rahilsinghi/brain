The email exchange documents the negotiation and finalization of employment terms between [[Fredrik Sjoberg]], CEO & Founder of [[Maison]], and [[Rahil Singhi]] for an [[AI Engineer]] role.

**Structured Offer Proposal:**
On April 13, 2026, Fredrik Sjoberg proposed a two-document approach to structure Rahil's engagement, primarily to simplify the process for his [[OPT/EAD]] work authorization, which was set to begin on June 22:
1.  **Offer Letter:** This document would confirm the full-time AI Engineer role starting June 22, including compensation details and the sign-on bonus, intended for submission to [[USCIS]].
2.  **Trial Period Agreement:** This separate agreement would cover the expectations and logistics for the interim period between April and June, serving as a mutual understanding for the ramp-up phase. Initially, Fredrik specified trial period compensation as $50/hour, up to 20 hours/week, to be integrated into the sign-on bonus upon full-time commencement.

**Confirmed Dates and Final Terms:**
[[Rahil Singhi]] confirmed the critical dates:
*   Trial period: Wednesday, April 16, 2026, through Friday, May 15, 2026.
*   Graduation: May 18, 2026.
*   Full-time start (OPT/EAD): June 22, 2026.

In the final communication on April 13, 2026, Fredrik specified the following definitive terms:

*   **Post-Graduation Availability:** Fredrik also inquired about Rahil's potential availability for sporadic involvement between May 18 and June 22, after graduation but before the official full-time start.
