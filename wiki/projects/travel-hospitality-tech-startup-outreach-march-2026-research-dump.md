# Travel & Hospitality Tech Startup Outreach — March 2026 Research Dump

A comprehensive research and outreach tracking document covering 27 new travel and hospitality tech companies identified in March 2026, spanning AI booking platforms, property management systems, revenue management, and guest experience tools. The document maps current outreach status across major OTAs and startups, identifies critical gaps in category coverage, and provides a phased activation plan through April 2026. Companies are tiered by skill match, hiring signals, and strategic fit with the candidate's background in MCP tooling, PMS integration, and LLM-based hotel distribution.

## Key Concepts

- **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**: Tool-calling standard used to expose hotel search, rates, and booking APIs to LLMs like ChatGPT and Gemini
- **OTA (Online Travel Agency)**: Platforms like Expedia, Airbnb, Booking.com, and KAYAK that aggregate and sell travel inventory
- **PMS (Property Management System)**: Core hotel operations software (e.g., Mews, Boom, Cloudbeds) managing reservations, check-in, and room inventory
- **Revenue Management**: Dynamic pricing and demand forecasting tools for hotels (e.g., Duetto, Lighthouse, RoomPriceGenie, FLYR)
- **GuestCX**: Guest communication and experience platforms (e.g., Canary Technologies, HiJiffy, Duve)
- **Agentic AI**: LLM-based autonomous systems that complete multi-step tasks such as booking, messaging, and task coordination without human input
- **Channel Management**: Systems that synchronize hotel inventory across multiple distribution platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO)
- **OHIP**: Oracle Hospitality Integration Platform, a PMS API standard for rate and reservation data
- **Tier A / Tier B / Tier C**: Internal prioritization tiers based on skill alignment, funding stage, hiring signals, and timing
- **5-Day Follow-Up Rule**: Outreach cadence guideline triggering follow-up emails five days after initial contact
- **PhocusWire Hot 25**: Annual industry list of top travel tech startups, used as a credibility and sourcing signal
