## Details

Brenton Andersen has proposed a Technical Co-founder role to Rahil Singhi for his project, aiming to enable significant early career impact and accommodate Rahil's existing commitments. The offer is structured as follows:

*   **Role**: Technical Co-founder
*   **Equity**: 12% with a 1-year cliff and 4-year vesting schedule.
*   **Sign-on Bonus**: $1,800 per month for time worked until the company raises $250,000 or more. Upon successful fundraising, the bonus will be paid out, and both parties will transition to full-time roles with determined salaries.
*   **Initial Commitment**: Approximately 10 hours per week during the pre-funding phase.

*   **Angel Round**: $250,000 - $500,000 on a $3 million valuation, resulting in an estimated equity value of $360,000.
*   **Pre-Seed Round**: $1 million - $1.2 million on an $8 million - $12 million valuation, resulting in an estimated equity value of $840,000 - $1.3 million.

This proposal aims to provide value and support Rahil's career goals while structuring the early-stage compensation in a manner favorable for future investment.
