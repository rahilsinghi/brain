## Details

The following information provides an overview of internal communications and strategic directives at [[Maison Labs]], primarily sourced from the `#all-maison` Slack channel.

### Rahil's Onboarding (April 14-15, 2026)
[[Rahil Singhi]] joined Maison Labs as an [[AI Engineer]] in April 2026, graduating from [[NYU]] in May. His prior experience includes work in the travel and hotel space. He was welcomed by several team members, including David, Kim, Javed, Sandeep, Durgansh, and Noel.

### Claude-Native Mandate (March 2, 2026)
[[Fredrik Sjoberg]] announced the official adoption of [[Claude]] for both Engineering and Commercial departments. This initiative, considered an "existential issue for Maison Labs," emphasizes an immediate transition to an AI-native approach.

The mandate includes three primary modes of Claude usage:
*   **Chat**: For general communication and queries.
*   **Cowork**: For collaborative work and content generation.
*   **Code**: For development and programming tasks.

A Pro plan at $20/month per person is expensed by Maison Labs, underscoring the company's commitment to this transition.

### March Goals
*   **Commercial Traction**: Accelerating the acquisition of paying clients.
*   **Product Velocity**: Increasing the speed of feature development and deployment, aiming for daily releases.

### Slack Rules (March 31, 2026)
[[Fredrik Sjoberg]] established guidelines for Slack usage to enhance structured and asynchronous communication, particularly to aid AI navigation of context:
*   Always comment in threads rather than directly in the channel.

### Weekly Cadence

*   **Thursdays**: Weekly product planning at 8:00 AM EST.
*   Full-team Claude sessions are held periodically.

### Key Events
