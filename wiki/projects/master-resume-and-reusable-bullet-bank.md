# Master Resume and Reusable Bullet Bank

This commit introduces a master resume source file and a reusable bullet bank to the Career-Datacenter repository, enabling rapid and targeted resume tailoring for various roles. The master resume consolidates all experience and project bullets with role-specific tailoring notes, while the bullet bank provides 41+ categorized, evidence-linked bullets for modular reuse. Together, these documents form a scalable system for customizing resumes across AI/ML, backend, data, and product domains.

## Key Concepts

- **Master Resume Source:** A comprehensive document containing all experience and project bullets, tech stack summaries, and tailoring notes for multiple role types
- **Bullet Bank:** A curated collection of 41+ reusable resume bullets organized by category and linked to evidence IDs
- **Resume Tailoring:** The practice of customizing resume content to match specific job roles or industries
- **Evidence-Linked Bullets:** Individual bullet points tied to verifiable evidence IDs for credibility and traceability
- **Role Categories:** AI/ML, Backend, Data, Cloud, SEO, Documentation, Leadership, and Research
- **Tech Stack Summaries:** Per-position overviews of technologies used, aiding quick role-specific selection

## Purpose and Workflow Integration

These two documents work together to enable a rapid resume tailoring workflow:

1. Identify the target role and its primary domain (e.g., AI/ML, Backend)
2. Consult `master_resume_source.md` for tailoring notes specific to that role type
3. Pull relevant bullets from `bullet_bank.md` using category tags
4. Verify bullet quality using the built-in checklist
5. Assemble a tailored resume from selected content

This system reduces the time required to customize a resume for each application while maintaining consistency and quality across versions.
