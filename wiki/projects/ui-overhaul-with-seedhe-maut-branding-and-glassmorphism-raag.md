# UI Overhaul with Seedhe Maut Branding and Glassmorphism (raag)

This commit details a comprehensive frontend redesign for the `raag` project, implementing a striking red, white, and black color scheme inspired by Seedhe Maut branding. The overhaul introduces glassmorphism cards, premium hover effects, and custom typography to create a visually engaging user interface.

## Details

A complete frontend redesign was implemented for the `raag` project, leveraging the distinctive brand palette of Seedhe Maut. The primary colors used are `#d91d1c` (red), `#050505` (black), and white, establishing a high-contrast and bold aesthetic.

Typography was carefully selected, with `KinetikaMaut` serving as the display font for headings to capture attention, and `TTCommonsPro` used for body text to ensure readability and a clean modern feel.

Key UI elements include:
*   **Glassmorphism Cards:** These translucent, frosted-glass-like cards are central to the new design, featuring premium hover effects that enhance interactivity and visual depth.
*   **Album Art Carousel:** An interactive carousel is displayed in the empty state, likely showcasing Seedhe Maut album covers, providing visual interest even when no content is active.
*   **SM Logo Integration:** The Seedhe Maut logo is prominently featured as the assistant avatar, reinforcing the brand identity throughout the application.
*   **Song Card Thumbnails:** Album thumbnails are integrated into song cards, offering a richer visual experience for individual tracks.
*   **Red-Accented Visuals:** Score rings and energy bars within the interface are accented with the signature red, tying together the visual theme and providing clear visual cues.
