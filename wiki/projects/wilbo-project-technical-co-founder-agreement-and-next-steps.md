## Details

This article details the agreement and initial discussions between [[Brenton Andersen Project Discussion|Brenton Andersen]] and [[rahil singhi]] regarding the [[Brenton's Project Wilbo and Associated Tools|Wilbo]] project.

**Initial Proposal from Brenton Andersen (April 14, 2026):**

Brenton offered Rahil the role of [[Technical Co-founder]] to have a meaningful impact and expedite career development. The proposal included:

*   **Equity**: 12% equity, with a standard 1-year cliff and 4-year vesting schedule.
*   **Compensation**: A sign-on bonus of $1,800 per month worked until a successful first round of $250k or greater is raised. Upon raising, Rahil would receive the sign-on bonus, and both would transition to full-time roles with salaries determined based on the raise.
*   **Time Commitment**: An estimated 10 hours per week initially, acknowledging that the first few months would involve minimal hours until funding is secured.
*   **Funding Goals (Ballpark Estimates)**:
    *   **Angel Round**: $250k - $500k on a $3m valuation, projecting Rahil's equity value at $360k.
    *   **Pre-Seed Round**: $1m - $1.2m on an $8m - $12m valuation, projecting Rahil's equity value at $840k - $1.3m.

**Rahil Singhi's Acceptance and Conditions (April 15, 2026):**

Rahil accepted the offer, confirming that "Technical Co-founder is the right framing." He expressed genuine interest in building the project beyond just clocking hours. Rahil raised several points for early consideration:

1.  **Legal Entity**: Flagged the eventual need for a [[US entity (LLC or C-Corp)]] for equity grants, cap table management, and [[OPT employment documentation]].
2.  **Tooling**: Requested coverage for a [[Claude Max]] subscription ($200/month) as a primary development tool for [[Wilbo]]'s pipelines.
3.  **Project Phases**: Indicated readiness to commence [[Phase 1 (data pipeline for screenwriting tool)]], proposing [[OpenRouter]] and [[Tiptap]] as the core stack, and offered to prepare a technical specification.
4.  **Immigration**: Noted that a technical co-founder role at a funded startup would provide strong evidence for an [[O-1 visa]], presenting a long-term immigration path alongside the [[OPT employment documentation|OPT]]/[[H-1B track]].

**Brenton Andersen's Response and Clarifications (April 15, 2026):**

Brenton was pleased with Rahil's acceptance and addressed his points:

1.  **Legal Entity**: Acknowledged the need to convert an existing [[LLC]] to a [[C-Corp]] for fundraising, noting it's not a significant cost if done early.
2.  **Tooling**: Agreed to cover the [[Claude Max]] subscription, pending confirmation of its sufficiency for the project's usage.
3.  **Project Workflow**: Suggested a call to discuss the workflow for the 3-phase pipeline, confirming shared vision for the project.
4.  **Relocation & Immigration**: Confirmed the goal for [[Wilbo]] to be NY-based, with Brenton planning relocation under an [[E3 visa]], expecting costs around $15k depending on the amount raised.
