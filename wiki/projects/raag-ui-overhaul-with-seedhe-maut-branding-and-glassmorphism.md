# Raag UI Overhaul with Seedhe Maut Branding and Glassmorphism

This commit details a comprehensive frontend redesign for the Raag project, incorporating the distinct red, white, and black brand palette of Seedhe Maut. It introduces a modern aesthetic with glassmorphism cards, premium hover effects, and custom typography to enhance the user experience and visual identity.

## Details

A complete frontend redesign was implemented for the `rahilsinghi/raag` repository (SHA: `1eeaec7`). The overhaul integrates the [[Seedhe Maut]] brand palette, featuring `#d91d1c` (red), `#050505` (black), and white as primary colors. For typography, `KinetikaMaut` was selected for headings and `TTCommonsPro` for body text.

The redesign emphasizes [[Glassmorphism]] cards with premium hover effects, contributing to a modern and interactive user interface. Specific UI elements were updated to reflect the new aesthetic and branding:
*   An album art carousel is displayed in the empty state.
*   The [[Seedhe Maut]] logo serves as the assistant avatar.
*   Album thumbnails are integrated into song cards.
*   Score rings and energy bars feature a distinctive red accent.

This update significantly enhances the visual appeal and brand consistency of the [[raag]] application.
