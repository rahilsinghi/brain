# Brain Project: Wiki Article Compilation and Embedding Enhancement

This update details the successful compilation of 12 raw articles using Claude Sonnet 4.6 and the embedding of all 97 existing wiki articles within the Brain project. A new helper script, `scripts/compile-pending.ts`, was introduced to streamline the compilation process, improving the project's knowledge base.

## Key Concepts

Raw article compilation,Wiki article embedding,LLM-powered content processing,Knowledge base management,Automation scripts

## Details

This commit for the `rahilsinghi/brain` repository marks a significant enhancement in the project's knowledge management capabilities:

*   **Raw Article Compilation**: 12 previously unstructured documents were successfully compiled into structured wiki articles using Claude Sonnet 4.6.
*   **Compiler Improvements**: The `max_tokens` limit for the compiler was increased to 8192, accommodating larger documents and ensuring comprehensive processing.
*   **Wiki Article Embedding**: All 97 existing wiki articles, comprising 603 chunks, have now been embedded, significantly expanding the project's navigable knowledge graph.
*   **New Helper Script**: A new utility script, `scripts/compile-pending.ts`, was added to facilitate and automate the compilation of pending raw content.

This work was co-authored by Claude Opus 4.6, highlighting the integrated use of LLMs in development workflows.
