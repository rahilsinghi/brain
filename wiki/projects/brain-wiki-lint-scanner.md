A new feature in the `rahilsinghi/brain` repository introduces a lint scanner to automatically detect and report issues within the wiki articles. This tool helps maintain the quality and consistency of the knowledge base by identifying broken links, orphaned content, and formatting errors.

## Details

The `rahilsinghi/brain` repository, at commit `18f731c` by Rahil Singhi on 2026-04-04, received an update to include a lint scanner. This scanner is designed to ensure the integrity and quality of the wiki articles stored in the brain knowledge base.

Its primary functions include:
*   **Detecting broken links**: Identifies internal and external links that no longer point to valid resources.
*   **Identifying orphan articles**: Flags articles that are not linked from any other part of the wiki, potentially indicating forgotten or isolated content.
*   **Checking for format issues**: Ensures consistent formatting across articles, improving readability and maintainability.
