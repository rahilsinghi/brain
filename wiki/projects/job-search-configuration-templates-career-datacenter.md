# Job Search Configuration Templates (Career-Datacenter)

This commit introduces essential configuration templates to the Career-Datacenter project, enabling systematic job tailoring and streamlined interview preparation. These templates cover job preferences, role definitions, keyword optimization for ATS, and behavioral interview stories.

## Details

This commit, `bfab505`, dated 2026-02-10, by Rahil Singhi in the `rahilsinghi/Career-Datacenter` repository, adds four critical YAML configuration files. These files are designed to standardize and automate various aspects of the job search and application process:

*   `preferences.yaml`: Defines personal job search constraints, desired company preferences, and compensation expectations, guiding the job filtering process.
*   `role_families.yaml`: Provides keyword mappings for various engineering roles such as backend, data, ML, quant, fullstack, and infrastructure, allowing for targeted job identification.
*   `keyword_library.yaml`: A comprehensive library for ATS optimization, mapping technical and soft skills to improve resume visibility.
*   `stories_star.yaml`: A template specifically for crafting behavioral interview stories using the STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) method, aiding in interview preparation.

These templates form a foundational layer for systematic job tailoring and efficient interview readiness within the [[Career Datacenter: Job Search Automation Platform]].
