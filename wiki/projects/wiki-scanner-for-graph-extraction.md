# Wiki Scanner for Graph Extraction

This feature introduces a wiki scanner capable of extracting nodes, links, and connection counts from wiki content within the `rahilsinghi/brain` repository. It automates the process of building a comprehensive knowledge graph by identifying relationships and entities from raw text.

## Key Concepts

## Details

This commit, `f6de304`, introduces a significant feature to the `rahilsinghi/brain` repository: a wiki scanner designed to systematically extract structural components of a knowledge graph. The scanner identifies and pulls out individual 'nodes' (representing concepts or entities), 'links' (representing relationships between nodes), and quantifies 'connection counts' (indicating the strength or number of relationships). This automation is crucial for building and maintaining a dynamic and comprehensive knowledge graph, laying the groundwork for advanced graph-based functionalities.
