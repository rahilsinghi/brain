# Master Resume and Reusable Bullet Bank — Career-Datacenter

This commit adds two foundational documents to the Career-Datacenter repository: a comprehensive master resume source file and a reusable bullet bank. Together, these resources enable rapid, targeted resume tailoring across a variety of technical roles including AI/ML, backend, data, and product positions. The bullet bank contains 41+ categorized, evidence-linked bullets to support consistent and verifiable resume content.

## Key Concepts

- **Master Resume Source**: A single comprehensive document containing all experience and project bullets, tech stack summaries, and tailoring notes for multiple role types
- **Bullet Bank**: A curated library of 41+ reusable resume bullets organized by category (AI/ML, Backend, Data, Cloud, SEO, Documentation, Leadership, Research)
- **Resume Tailoring**: The practice of customizing resume content to match specific job descriptions and target roles
- **Evidence Linking**: Each bullet in the bank is linked to an evidence ID, ensuring claims are verifiable and traceable
- **Role-Based Targeting**: Tailoring notes guide adaptation of content for AI/ML, backend, data, and product roles specifically

## Purpose and Workflow

These two documents form a resume infrastructure system:
1. The **master resume** provides the raw material — all possible bullets and context.
2. The **bullet bank** distills the best bullets into an indexed, categorized format.
3. Together, they allow rapid assembly of tailored resumes without starting from scratch for each application.
