# CUNY Sustainability and Green Tech Virtual Graduate Fair

The City University of New York invites prospective students to attend a virtual graduate fair focused on sustainability and green technology programs. The event, scheduled for Wednesday, April 22, offers insights into graduate pathways in Environmental Engineering, Urban Sustainability, Climate Policy, and Geographic Information Science.

## Key Concepts

- Graduate education in sustainability
- Virtual career fair
- Environmental Engineering programs
- Urban Sustainability pathways
- Climate Policy degrees
- Green technology sector opportunities

## Details

### Event Overview
The CUNY Sustainability and Green Tech Virtual Graduate Fair provides an opportunity to explore graduate programs across the university that focus on sustainability and green technology. Held on **Wednesday, April 22, from 6:00 PM to 7:15 PM EST** via Zoom, the event is designed for students interested in environmental fields and careers that contribute to a sustainable future.

### Key Features
- **Faculty Engagement**: Direct interaction with faculty and program directors to learn about sustainability degree options.
- **Admissions Insights**: Guidance on the application process and career opportunities in the green technology sector.
- **Program-Specific Breakouts**: Department representatives will be available to address specific program inquiries and help attendees identify the best fit for their goals.

### Registration
To secure your spot, register at [https://welcome.cuny.edu/register/?id=ffeb7c54-297a-4ae1-8e0b-e78621ab3f4b](https://welcome.cuny.edu/register/?id=ffeb7c54-297a-4ae1-8e0b-e78621ab3f4b).

### Contact Information
For questions, reach out to the CUNY Office of Graduate Studies at [grad@cuny.edu](mailto:grad@cuny.edu) or by phone at 646-664-3550.

This event aligns with CUNY's mission to prepare students for impactful careers in sustainability and environmental fields.
