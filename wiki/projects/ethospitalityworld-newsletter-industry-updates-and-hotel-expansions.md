## Details

- **Royal Orchid Hotels and Hamptons by Hilton**: Arjun Baljee, president of Royal Orchid Hotels Limited (ROHL), emphasized that the partnership fills a mid-market gap and accelerates development.
- **Civil Aviation Ministry Update**: The Ministry reduced landing and parking charges at non-major airports to curb rising airfares, benefiting both passengers and airlines.
- **Wyndham Hotels Expansion**: Wyndham is expanding its presence in emerging Indian markets, focusing on destinations with strong domestic travel demand.
- **TreeHouse Hotel in Moradabad**: TreeHouse Hotels announced a new full-service hotel in Moradabad, catering to modern travelers' needs.
- **Leadership Appointments**: Accor appointed Amit Malik as vice president of people and culture for South Asia, while Hyatt Regency Ahmedabad promoted Aashish Sharma to director of sales.
- **Restaurant Expansions**: Millo expanded to Nagpur, and Wow! Momo reached 850 stores across India, reflecting growing urban food service demand.
