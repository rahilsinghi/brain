Given that your work authorization kicks in on June 22, I think the cleanest approach is to split this into two short documents rather than one:
An offer letter: This would confirm your full-time AI Engineer role starting June 22, your compensation, and the sign-on bonus. This is the document you’d use for USCIS, and it gives you the written commitment you asked for.
Trial period agreement: covering expectations and logistics for the April–June period while you’re getting up to speed with the team and codebase. Think of it as a mutual understanding of what the ramp-up looks like before you officially start. Please confirm your dates again.
