# Maison Competitive Landscape
**Source:** Slack #competitors channel + competitor deep dives
**Retrieved:** April 15, 2026

## Key Competitors

### Guest Messaging / Chatbot
- **HiJiffy** (Portugal) — 2,500 hotels. Mature guest comms hub + unified inbox. WEAKNESS: Poor conversational AI, slow setup, fragmented backend. Pricing: €159/mo multi-channel, €249/mo with PMS integration.
- **Runnr.ai** (Netherlands) — Fast-moving Benelux challenger. Proactive upsell messaging claiming €30-50/room/month. WEAKNESS: smaller scale, unproven claims.
- **Asksuite** — #1 Hotel Chatbot at Hotel Tech Awards 2026
- **Quinta** (formerly Quicktext) — Rebranded Jan 2026, pivoted from chatbot to full AI data infrastructure

### Full-Stack Hotel Tech
- **Canary Technologies** — 20,000+ hotels, $180M raised, ~$600M valuation. AI-first ops platform: check-in/out, guest messaging (80-90% automated), upsell. Launched Hospitality AI Agent Studio (March 2026). Brand standard at Marriott, Hilton, Four Seasons, Wyndham.
- **Revinate** — 12,500+ hotels, $130M raised, $18.9M ARR (+27% YoY). Owns guest data layer — 950M+ rich profiles. Email marketing (20:1 ROI), AI messaging (Ivy), voice/reservation sales. New CEO Bryson Koehler (ex-IHG). 2025 CDP upgrade caused customer pain = opportunity.

### AI Visibility Specific
- **Lighthouse (Connect AI)** — ChatGPT app for hotels. Live rates → direct booking in ChatGPT. 12-18 month window claim.
- **Cendyn (AI Connect)** — "visible in AI search"
- **Profound** — tracking AI visibility, mentions, citations
- **80Days** — 3Cs framework (Clarity, Consistency, Credibility)

## Competitive Categories
- Transactional outbound messaging: Revinate, Canary
- Campaign messaging: Revinate, Cendyn
- Inbound guest messaging / Unified inbox: Canary, HiJiffy, Runnr, Akia
- Service request management: ALICE, INTELITY

## Maison's Competitive Advantage
- Superior conversational AI quality
- KEY GAP: Unified inbox (top priority feature to build)
- KEY GAP: Tangible ROI metrics (need attribution data)
- Opportunity: Booking.com API integration (now meet 100-hotel minimum)

## Strategic Takeaway
Competitors are feature-rich but AI-weak. Maison must match feature breadth (especially unified inbox) while leveraging superior AI quality.
