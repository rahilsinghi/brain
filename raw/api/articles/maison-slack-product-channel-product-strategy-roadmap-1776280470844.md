# Maison Slack #product — Product Strategy & Roadmap
**Source:** Slack #product channel (C08H3A3Q14L)
**Retrieved:** April 15, 2026

## Product Vision — Three Agents Framework
Maison positions as a **commercial operating system** for hotels built around three complementary agents:

### 1. Marketing Agent (AI visibility + content flywheel)
- Turns hotel's approved content into source of truth
- Enables AI-native conversion: guests discover, evaluate, complete direct booking inside personal AI
- Flags what's missing/unclear, recommends what to publish next (FAQs, policies, structured data)

### 2. Sales Agent (conversion + lead capture)
- Reduces friction on path to booking
- Captures high-intent inquiries: groups, weddings, meetings, buyouts, longer stays
- Turn consideration into qualified leads or bookings

### 3. Support Agent (deflection + workload reduction)
- Handles repetitive pre-stay questions (parking, check-in/out, pet policy, breakfast, etc.)
- Instant answers in guest's language, fewer staff interruptions

## API-First Future
- "API-first is the future of Maison Labs" — Fredrik

## Key Product Discussions
- **Agents terminology** — internal (Tool → Skill → Agent) vs external (Pre-built Agent → Custom Agent)
- **Schema markup / JSON-LD** — critical for AI visibility, training needed across teams
- **WebMCP** — browser's ability to use website as MCP
- **Lead capture** — hotels increasingly requesting contact capture functionality
- **Source ingestion** — auto-ingest booking.com and Expedia profiles to enrich KB
- **PMS marketplace integrations** — Cloudbeds certified, Mews certified, Opera Cloud in progress
